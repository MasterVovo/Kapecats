About Us

What Makes Our Coffee Special?

At KapeCats, we’re all about combining our love for coffee and cats into a cozy, welcoming experience. Since our establishment in 2021, we’ve been serving up delightful drinks made with care and passion. Our coffee is crafted from 100% Arabica Sagada beans, celebrated for their rich flavor and smooth aroma. For our cold brew enthusiasts, we steep every batch for 20 hours to bring out the perfect balance of bold and mellow notes. We also use a medium roast to ensure each cup highlights the natural sweetness and nuances of the beans. Whether you're here for a quick caffeine fix, to relax with friends, or to enjoy our cat-inspired vibe, KapeCats promises a sip worth savoring.
